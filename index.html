About

HOLLYWOOD STEAMERS — professional furniture cleaning company in Los Angeles.

“We understand that you are letting us into the most valuable place — your home. That’s why at HOLLYWOOD STEAMERS we build relationships based on honesty, care, and attention to detail.”

We serve clients from Santa Monica to Laguna Beach, offering high-quality cleaning of sofas, armchairs, and other types of furniture.

Our mission is to make your home safe and healthy. We use only modern equipment and eco-friendly products that are safe for children and pets, as well as modern equipment.

The company was founded by Sergei, a specialist with extensive experience working in leading cleaning companies in the United States. Having received formal education in the field of furniture cleaning, he developed his own system that meets the latest environmental standards and delivers impeccable quality.
